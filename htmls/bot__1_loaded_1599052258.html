Dunk Low Disrupt

Black
$110
Available 9/4 at 4:00 PM

Recent times have seen a spotlight focused on the Dunk, with the silhouette appearing in a plethora of makeups ranging from groundbreaking collaborations to heritage-inspired retros. To extend the look’s legacy, we now introduce the Dunk Low Disrupt—a ladies-only, modern take on the legendary design that uses a platform stance and exaggerated proportions to create a uniquely feminine feel. Extra dimension around the outsole and upper panels add new energy to the shoe’s enduring design lines, while familiar details like a low-cut, padded collar support everyday wearability. This edition sports a contrasting color palette featuring shades of summit white and black.
